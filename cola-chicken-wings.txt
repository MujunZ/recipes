Ingredients:
 - 2 cups of cola
 - 1/4 cups of dark soy souse
 - 1 pound small chicken wings (about 10)

Directions:
 1. Stir fry the chicken wings with high heat
 
 2. Add dark soy souse and stir fry quickly
 
 3. Add cola and turn to mid-low heat. Cover the lid and wait for 15 to 20 minutes.

 4, If there are still a lot of liquid, turn to high heat till its only condensed souse left
